Reception

The film received a rating of 4 out of 5 in Horror Society.[1] Mark L.
Miller of Ain't It Cool News wrote a positive review of the film, writing that
"if you’re not the type to nitpick things like plot holes and lack of motivation,
you’re bound to have a good time with this gory slasher yarn."[2]
